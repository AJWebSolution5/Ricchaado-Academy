About Ricchaado

Richard is a 24 year old creative talent (if he so says himself) from London, U.K. He is most recognized for his comedy - online fluent Japanese skits that portray the funny reality of living in Japan as a foreigner. In his late teens, Richard was bored enough to move to Japan and find a job on purely self taught Japanese and now wants to share knowledge and culture with other people who are starting their Japanese Journey!"

About Bruno

Hey there! I'm Bruno. Richard and I met in Japan – we were both living there at the same time and we had friends in common. I worked there as a teacher and opened my own language school in my home country, Brazil. So we Richard told me about Ricchaado Academy I instantly wanted to be a part of it! I handle all of the administrative stuff – reports, bills, letters and certificates, basically any demand our students might have, I'm the guy to talk to. In my spare time, I like to travel to places I haven't been before, spend time with my family (wife, dog and cat!), and, well, study Japanese! I'm not at Richard's level yet, but I'll get there.

OUR MISSION

The ideology behind Ricchaado Academy is to show that attaining Japanese proficiency is a lot more accessible than people think and living in Japan is not "necessarily necessary". Richard is excited for RA school to play a significant role in your overall growth because according to him "you're not really gonna find a better vibe than this.
